Company History

Started in 1992 as a start up company, Arts and Creatives was started by the Whilby Family who strived in Fine Art, Scultpture and Fashion. Throughout the years as technology and the world evolved, they expanded their mediums to include 3D artists, graphic designers, writers and even glass blowing. Arts and Creatives had received funding from kickstarter grants in 2012 and was able to open their compamy to the stock market and allow shareholders to provide perks and benefits to their members. Today Arts and Creatives is the only and largest Creative Support Group that works globally.
